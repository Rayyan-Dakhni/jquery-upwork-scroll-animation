It takes 22 subcontractors to build the average home.
A disjointed process.

This siloed approach leaves room for error at every turn. While expertise and efficiency may be the driving claims, advanced technology offers new ways to meet, exceed and ultimately raise standards.

An Onx home is constructed as 1system.
A coordinated process.

From start to finish, every aspect of an Onx home is part of a holistic design process perfected by cutting-edge technology. The result is impeccable construction imbued with a deep reverence for the details that give a home its innate beauty.

X⁺ CONSTRUCTION™

Design+

Onx builds homes holistically as a single integrated system, giving homeowners a higher standard of living space. Raising the standard, Onx core homes come with unique design features like high ceilings, tall impact-proof windows and high-end finishes, creating an elevated design aesthetic, bringing more value to customers and eliminating shortcuts and disappointments faced in traditional construction.

Our holistic design process streamlines the work that’s typically done by 22 subcontractors over 7 months into 1 cohesive system that comes together in under 60 days.

3x Faster

Bathroom Pod+

Smart designs like our Bathroom Pod⁺ construction system come standard with an Onx home. Advanced engineered pods are manufactured in a controlled facility and combine high-performing mechanical, electrical and plumbing systems with beautifully designed fixtures and smart design features. This saves time and costs, which are directly reinvested into the rest of the home.

Due to tech-enabled efficiencies like our bathroom pod system, Onx homes have up to 20% more value at a comparable price.

20% Value

Core Walls+

Built to withstand 175 mph wind loads for hurricane protection, Onx Core Walls⁺ protect your home against the toughest weather. Core Walls⁺ are sandwiched with specialized insulation that absorbs sound, creating a serene environment for your family.

Our concrete walls boast above average strength of 6000 psi, ensuring high durability and protection for your home.

6000 psi

Staircase+

As opposed to traditional wood plank flooring and stairs, Onx builds floors and staircases with concrete reinforced with prestressed beams. This not only creates a sturdier structure, but provides a greater separation of sound and space between floors, and stairs that will never creak.

The higher the Sound Transmission Class (STC), the quieter the living space—our concrete has an STC rating above 49, and reduces sound by 80% in comparison to wood-frame construction.

80% Quieter
